# Proposal: Less Intrusive Overview Tick Marks
**Status: Ready for Review**

## Intent
The bar marker lines on the overview waveform are full-height, which makes them visually compete with the waveform itself. They should be subtle positional guides, not dominant features.

## Specification Deltas

### MODIFIED
- Overview bar markers are rendered as short notches at the bottom of the overview panel rather than full-height lines, so they are visible as positional guides without obscuring the waveform above them.
